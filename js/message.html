It is a matter of immense pleasure informing you that, we are organizing a Two Week Workshop and Summer School on “Advances in Deep Architectures for Signal, Image & Vision Applications” during July 14, 2018, to July 28, 2018.
The objectives of the workshop are highlighted as follows -

  1. Building awareness towards deep learning architectures.
  2. Building communities of research students, educator, R&D, and Industry persons in this emerging area of research and development.
  3. Providing hands-on tutorial sessions, where the participants can experiment with concepts and methods.
  4. Building the technical capacity in the area of Signal, Image, and Vision Processing via deep architectures.
  5. Providing a platform to showcase the research work through the Technical paper and poster presentation.
All of us are aware of the fact that the Deep Learning domain is quite thrilling now a days and many researchers (students/faculty members/industries) started doing research in this important domain, but very little expertise is available in India. In ADASIVA-2018, 17 prominent speakers have confirmed their participation uptil now. The details of the summer school can be found at https://cvbl.iiita.ac.in/adasiva2018/.
